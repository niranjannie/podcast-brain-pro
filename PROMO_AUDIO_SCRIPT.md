### 1. Education & E-Learning

### 2. News & Journalism
- Newsrooms auto-generate audio briefings from written articles
- Regional journalists create Kannada, Hindi, Tamil news podcasts
- Daily digest shows produced automatically every morning

### 3. Content Creators & Influencers
- YouTubers repurpose scripts into podcast episodes
- bloggers scale from text to audio without recording equipment
- Podcast networks batch-produce episodes across multiple topics

### 4. Enterprise & Internal Comms
- Companies create training audio in multiple languages
- HR departments generate onboarding podcasts
- Sales teams produce product explainer audio at scale

- Create audio versions of documentation and help articles
- Generate screen-reader-friendly spoken content

- Build voice-enabled apps with local TTS (no API costs)
- Create voice assistants with custom personas
- Integrate into CMS platforms for automatic audio generation
